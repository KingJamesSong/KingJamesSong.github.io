Yue Song

I am a second-year Ph.D. student with Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG) at the University of Trento, Italy, advised by Nicu Sebe.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, and numerical analysis and optimization. Recently I work on investigating efficient and robust numerical methods in computer vision applications.

Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received the B.Sc. cum laude from KU Leuven, Belgium and the joint M.Sc. summa cum laude from the University of Trento, Italy and KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, co-advised by Nicu Sebe and Kevin Smith. Besides the technical master's degree, I received an Innovation & Entrepreneurship minor degree from European Institute of Innovation and Technology (EIT Digital).
